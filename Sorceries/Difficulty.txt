Difficulty

Choose an opponent. They choose one:

Beginner: Owner of this card draws two cards.

Normal: Owner of this card searches their library for a creature with converted mana cost 4 or less. They put it on top of their library, then shuffle.

You get 3 colorless mana.

Hard: Owner of this card makes two copies of a creature they control.

Search your library for an artifact or enchantment. Put it in your hand, then shuffle.
