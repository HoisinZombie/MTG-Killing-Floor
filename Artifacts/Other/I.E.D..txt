I.E.D.

When I.E.D. enters, all players roll a 6 sided die. Whichever player rolls the lowest gains control of this card.

If I.E.D. was on your field this turn, when 3 or more creatures you control die in a single turn, play a creature from your hand without paying its cost. Then, trigger Zed Time 1.

{t}, 3 colorless: Sacrifice I.E.D. Destroy all creatures you control.
